Who am I?

I am a qualified and motivated Industrial Engineer who found with the PhD a real passion for Computer Science. My PhD about knowledge representation in Industry 4.0 to enhance interoperability has initialised me in the world of AI (prior to the hype of LLM). I have experience in the automation industry and a wide interest in new developments. I am multilingual, a native Spanish speaker, and have a high level of English and Italian. I am keenly interested in multidisciplinary knowledge with the common link of improvements and interconnect data. I am confident about new challenges or tight deadlines.
